Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Span does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter into the app — including events, tasks, and preferences — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Span does not require an account, does not use analytics or tracking tools, and does not share data with third parties.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to delete or request. If you uninstall the app, all data is removed with it.
